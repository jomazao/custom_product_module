Installation

  1. Purchase and download the Custom Product Module from the Odoo App Store or any authorized source.
  2. Go to the Apps menu in your Odoo instance.
  3. Click on the "Apps" tab and then the "Update Apps List" button.
  4. Search for "Custom Product Module" and click "Install".
  5. Once installed, navigate to the "Products" section to configure the module settings.
